Undergraduate Academic Programs

Comprehensive B.Eng Agricultural and Environmental Engineering Program designed to produce competent engineers for sustainable agricultural development.

B.Eng Agricultural & Environmental Engineering

A comprehensive 5-year undergraduate program that integrates engineering principles with agricultural and environmental sciences to address challenges in food production, environmental sustainability, and rural development.

Graduation Requirements

Academic and professional standards that must be met to earn the B.Eng degree.

B.Eng Agricultural & Environmental Engineering — Graduation Standards

University of Ibadan · Faculty of Technology · 2024/2025 Session

Academic Requirements
  • Minimum of 180 credit units completed
  • Minimum CGPA of 1.50 on a 5.0 scale
  • Successful completion of SIWES (6 months)
  • Satisfactory final year project defense
  • No outstanding financial obligations
  • Cleared by all relevant departments
Professional Development
  • Eligible for COREN graduate registration
  • Verified industry internship experience
  • Demonstrated research presentation skills
  • Technical report writing proficiency
  • Teamwork and engineering leadership
  • Adherence to engineering ethics code

Career Prospects

Our graduates pursue diverse and impactful career paths across multiple sectors of the economy.

Engineering Consulting
Design and consultation services for agricultural systems, irrigation projects, and environmental solutions for public and private clients.
Agricultural Technology
Development of smart farming solutions, precision agriculture systems, and innovative agricultural machinery for modern food production.
Environmental Engineering
Environmental impact assessment, waste management systems, water quality management, and sustainable development project planning.
Renewable Energy
Bioenergy, solar-powered irrigation, and renewable energy systems tailored to the agricultural and rural development sector.
Research & Academia
Academic research, teaching at universities, and leading R&D initiatives in agricultural engineering and environmental science institutions.
Government & Policy
Technical roles in government ministries, agricultural development programs (ADPs), and international development organizations like FAO and IITA.
